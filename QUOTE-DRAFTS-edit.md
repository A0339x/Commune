### Themes
- Acknowledge what happened without dwelling
- No judgment for selling — "we all felt that", "who wouldn't?", "that's human"
- Be honest about panic selling — most selling happened during drops
- Mention the euphoria/excitement — not just the crashes
- Euphoria = excitement going UP, not chaos
- **Smooth transitions** — use "throughout all of that" to connect story to community
- Recognize older holders — define who they are, how long they've been here
- "Believed in the project" not just "believed"
- "Conviction in the project" not just "conviction"
- Invite them to chat — not asking questions, inviting to share/connect
- Welcome language for new members — "you're part of the family now", "glad you're here"
- Learning curve acknowledgment — "everyone goes through this", "we've all been there"
- Hope through community connection, not promises
- Diamond Grip holders — "buy something you believe in, hold, focus on other things"
- Be specific — mention consecutive buy months, specific events, percentages
