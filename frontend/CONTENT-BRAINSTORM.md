## Scene Flow Logic

### Scene 0: Title
Always shows. No variation needed.

### Scene 1: Date (First purchase)
**Content varies by:**
- If Founding Member: "July 15, 2021 - Day One"
- If Early Adopter: "You were early. [Date]"
- Regular: "Your GUARD journey began [Date]"

**Duration stat:**
- "[X] days ago"
- "[X] years and [Y] months"

### Scene 2: Accumulator
**Skip if:** Single purchase holder

**Content varies by:**
- all_in style: "One decisive move"
- dca_master: "The art of accumulation"
- steady_stacker: "Stack by stack"
- occasional: "Strategic entries"

**Stats:**
- Total buys
- Biggest buy amount
- Total accumulated

### Scene 3: Timing
**Content varies by timing quality:**
- Best buy near ATL: "Perfect timing exists. You found it."
- Best buy during major dip: "You bought the dip. Literally."
- Worst buy near ATH: Include gentle humor about learning experience

### Scene 4: Paper Hands (CONDITIONAL)
**Only show if:** hasPaperHanded === true (76% of users)
**Skip if:** Diamond Hands

**Content varies by intensity:**
- 1-2 sells: Light redemption arc
- 3-10 sells: Moderate comeback story
- 10+ sells: Heavy trading journey

**Always end positive:**
- "But you came back."
- "And now you're holding [X] GUARD"

### Scene 5: Style
**Based on buyingStyle field:**
- dca_master: "DCA runs in your veins"
- steady_stacker: "Slow and steady wins"
- all_in: "When you move, you move big"
- occasional: "Quality over quantity"

### Scene 6: Badges + Quote
**Order badges by priority:**
1. Whale (if applicable)
2. Founding Member (if applicable)
3. Early Adopter
4. Diamond Hands
5. True Believer
6. DCA Master
7. Steady Stacker
8. Comeback Kid

**Quote selection based on primary badge**

### Scene 7: Username
Color based on badge tier (already in presentationConfig.js)

### Scene 8: Summary
**Stats to include:**
- Total GUARD held
- Primary badge
- Days as holder
- Number of transactions
